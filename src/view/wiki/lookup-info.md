## Reset Mechanic Formulas

### Prestige

$$db = \frac{\log_10^0.8{\left(f(t)\right)}}{4*10^6}$$

$$d\mu = \log_10{\left(f(t)\right)}$$

### Supremacy

$$d\psi = 2^{\frac{\log_10{\left(\log_10{\left(f(t)\right)}\right)}}{25} - 1} - 0.5$$

### Graduation

A new graduation is only possible upon reaching a new maximum $F(t)$ and when $d\sigma>0$. The total number of students, $\sigma$, is calculated as follows:

$$\sigma = \frac{\log_10{\left(\log_10{\left(f(t)\right)}\right)}}{200} - 5$$

###### Note: This is equivalent to saying you get a new student every ee200 $F(t)$, starting at ee2000 $F(t)$.
